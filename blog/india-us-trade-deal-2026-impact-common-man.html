📋 1. India-US BTA — Current Status Kya Hai?

February 2026 mein PM Modi aur President Trump ki call ke baad interim trade framework announce hua. Phir SCOTUS ne IEEPA tariffs ko Feb 20 ko illegal declare kar diya — jisse India pe 10% Section 122 surcharge aa gaya. Abhi formally BTA (Bilateral Trade Agreement) ke negotiations chal rahe hain commerce ministry level pe.

Feb 2, 2026
Modi-Trump Call — bilateral trade deal announced. Both sides committed to $500B trade target by 2030.
Feb 6-7, 2026
White House joint statement — framework signed. Specific sectors: IT, pharma, defense, agriculture.
Feb 20, 2026
SCOTUS ruling — IEEPA tariff illegal. India-US deal urgency reduced slightly — but negotiations ongoing.
March 2026
Commerce secretary level meetings — technical discussions. Agriculture, digital services tax, IPR — main sticking points.
Jul 24, 2026 — Deadline
Section 122 expires — BTA ya Section 301 targeted tariffs. Biggest decision point.

💻 3. IT Sector Pe Kya Hoga — H1-B, Visas, Market Access

India ka IT sector $60 billion US services export karta hai — yeh tariff exempt hai. Lekin trade deal se bhi IT ko direct benefits hain:

  • H1-B Visa quota: Deal mein India-specific H1-B expansion negotiate ho raha hai — 65,000 → potentially 85,000-90,000
  • Digital services tax: India ki equalization levy (2% digital tax) remove karni padegi US demand pe
  • Data localization: US companies India mein operate karne ke liye data storage rules relax chahte hain
  • Market access expansion: Indian IT companies US government contracts ke liye eligible hone ka scope
✅ IT Sector Bottom Line: Trade deal se IT sector ko net positive impact milega — more visas, more market access. IT services tariff-exempt already hain. Deal se beyond-tariff benefits milenge: visa, data, contracts. Estimated 2-3 lakh additional IT jobs in 3 years post deal.

💊 4. Pharma Impact — Generic Medicines Ka Kya Hoga?

India world's largest supplier of generic medicines hai aur US ka biggest pharma exporter hai. ~$8 billion pharma exports to US annually. US generic drug market India pe heavily dependent hai — 40% US generics India se aate hain.

📊 Why Pharma Won't Get Heavy Tariffs: US patients depend on Indian generics for affordable healthcare. Imposing heavy tariffs on Indian pharma would raise US drug prices significantly — politically very unpopular. Even in worst-case scenario, pharma gets special treatment. Most analysts expect pharma to remain largely exempt.

🔄 5. India Ka Kya Milega, Kya Dena Padega?

Trade deal ek-sided nahi hota — dono side ko give and take karna padta hai. India ka likely negotiating position:

India Will Give (Concessions):

  • Import duties reduce on US goods: Harley-Davidson bikes, almonds, apples, chicken
  • Equalization levy (2% digital tax) remove — US tech companies (Google, Meta, Amazon) direct beneficiary
  • IPR protections strengthen — pharma patents, software copyrights
  • Medical devices market access ease karna — US devices on India priority list

India Expects to Get:

  • Zero/reduced tariffs on manufactured goods: textiles, gems, engineering
  • IT services market access expansion + more H1-B visas
  • US pharma market access secure rakhna + USFDA fast-track
  • Defense technology transfer cooperation

❓ 8. FAQs — Trade Deal Ke Common Sawal

India-US trade deal ka aam aadmi pe kya impact hoga?
Trade deal se aam aadmi ko: US products saste ho sakte hain (Apple products, almonds, apples), IT jobs safe rahenge, pharma price stable rahega, aur India ke US exports badhenge — jisse GDP growth aur jobs benefit honge. Deal na hua toh targeted tariffs se specific goods mahnge ho sakte hain.
India-US deal ke baad iPhone price India mein gir sakti hai?
Partially haan. Agar India Apple products pe import duty reduce karta hai aur US India pe tariff hata deta hai, toh iPhone India mein 10-15% sasta ho sakta hai. Apple ka India manufacturing expansion (Made in India iPhone from Foxconn Tamil Nadu) bhi price help karta hai — local manufacturing = lower import duty applicable.
Trade deal se IT sector jobs pe kya asar hoga?
Trade deal se IT sector directly benefit karta hai. H1-B visa rules relax hone se IT professionals US mein easy placement milti hai. India-US digital trade framework se IT services market access expand hogi. Estimated 2-3 lakh additional IT jobs in next 3 years post deal sign hone ke baad.
July 2026 deadline ke baad kya hoga agar deal nahi hua?
July 24, 2026 ke baad agar BTA finalize nahi hua, most likely Section 122 extension hogi (base case). Worst case mein Section 301 targeted tariffs kick in ho sakte hain — India ke specific sectors (digital services, pharma patents, gems) pe. Short-term market volatility expected, lekin long-term negotiations continue rahenge.
India-US trade deal mein India ko kya dena hoga?
India ko likely: import duties reduce karni hongi US products pe (Harley Davidson, agricultural products, medical devices), digital services tax (equalization levy) remove karni hogi, aur IPR protections strengthen karni hongi. India return mein mangega: IT services market access, more H1-B visas, pharma export security, and defense tech transfer.
