📐 EMI Formula — How Banks Calculate Your EMI

Har bank same formula use karta hai — chahe SBI ho ya HDFC. EMI ka matlab hai Equated Monthly Installment — ek fixed amount jo aap har mahine bank ko pay karte ho, jismein principal + interest dono included hota hai.

📌 EMI Formula:
EMI = P × r × (1+r)ⁿ ÷ [(1+r)ⁿ − 1]

Where:
P = Principal loan amount (₹50,00,000)
r = Monthly interest rate (Annual rate ÷ 12 ÷ 100)
n = Total number of months (Years × 12)

Example: ₹50 Lakh loan at SBI's 8.25% for 20 years → r = 8.25/12/100 = 0.006875, n = 240 months. Plug into formula → EMI = ₹42,649/month. Total payment = ₹1,02,35,760 — matlab interest alone ₹52.36 Lakh! Is liye prepayment bohot zaroori hai (section 4 mein detail hai).

✅ RBI Repo Rate Update (March 2026): RBI ne repo rate 5.25% par set kiya hai (source: rbi.org.in). Jab repo rate girta hai, banks bhi apne home loan rates kam karte hain — is liye 2026 mein rates relatively competitive hain.

📋 Loan Eligibility — FOIR Rule Samjho

Bank aapko loan dega ya nahi — ye FOIR (Fixed Obligation to Income Ratio) se decide hota hai. Simply put: aapki total EMIs (home loan + car loan + credit card + personal loan) aapki monthly income ke 50-60% se zyada nahi honi chahiye.

📌 FOIR Formula:
FOIR = (All Monthly EMIs ÷ Monthly Income) × 100

Banks usually approve if FOIR ≤ 50-55%. Agar FOIR > 60%, loan reject ho sakta hai.

🎯 Tips for Getting the Lowest Home Loan Rate

✅ DO THIS — Rate Kam Hoga

✅ CIBIL Score 750+ rakhein — best rates milte hain
✅ 20%+ down payment do — LTV kam = lower risk = better rate
✅ Salary account wahi bank mein ho jahan se loan le rahe ho
✅ Woman co-applicant add karo — 0.05% extra discount milta hai
✅ Shorter tenure choose karo (15 vs 20 yr) — less interest
✅ Balance transfer karo agar current bank ki rate zyada hai
VS

❌ AVOID THIS — Rate Badh Jaayega

❌ Multiple loan applications ek saath — hard inquiries se CIBIL girti hai
❌ Credit card outstanding chhoda — poor repayment history
❌ Job switch loan apply karte time — 1 year stability chahiye
❌ Under-construction property pe max loan — risk premium lagta hai
❌ Processing fees compare nahi karna — some banks charge 0.5%+
❌ Fixed rate choose karna — floating rate almost always cheaper

🔄 Home Loan Balance Transfer — Kab Karna Chahiye?

Agar aapka current home loan rate 9%+ hai aur market mein 7.45-8.25% mil raha hai, toh balance transfer se lakhs bach sakte hain. Lekin balance transfer tabhi faydemand hai jab:

✅ Balance Transfer Checklist:
✓ Rate difference at least 0.50% ho
✓ Remaining tenure 10+ years ho (zyada tenure = zyada savings)
✓ Processing + legal fees milake bhi net savings positive ho
✓ New bank mein better service & digital experience ho
